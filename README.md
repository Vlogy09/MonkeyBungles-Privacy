MonkeyBungles Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1/17/2026

This application (“the App”) is a virtual reality game developed for Meta Quest.

Information We Collect
The App accesses the device microphone to enable real-time in-game voice chat between players. Audio data is processed in real time for communication purposes.

How We Use Information
Microphone input is used solely to provide in-game voice communication. Audio is not recorded, stored, or used for any purpose other than enabling voice chat during gameplay.

Data Sharing
Audio data may be transmitted to other players or voice service providers solely to enable real-time voice communication. No audio data is sold or shared for advertising purposes.

User Control
Microphone access is only active while the App is running and voice chat is enabled. Users can disable voice chat or revoke microphone permission through their device settings.

Children’s Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
